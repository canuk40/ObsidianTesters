# Privacy Policy — ObsidianTesters

**Effective Date:** February 25, 2025  
**Last Updated:** February 25, 2025

ObsidianTesters ("we", "us", or "our") is operated by the ObsidianTesters team. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the ObsidianTesters mobile application (the "App").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Google Account Information** — When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google OAuth.
- **App Listings** — When you post an app for testing, we collect your app name, developer name, description, Play Store link, Google Group email, and any app icon image you upload.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Shard Balance & Transactions** — We track your shard balance and transaction history to operate the platform economy.
- **Test Progress** — We record which apps you have tested and when, including timer completion data.
- **Device & Crash Data** — We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and ANR data to improve app stability. This may include device model, OS version, and app state at the time of a crash.

### 1.3 Payment Information
- **In-App Purchases** — Purchases are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment card details. We only receive a confirmation of purchase status from Google Play.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Authenticate your identity and maintain your account
- Display your profile, shard balance, and app listings within the App
- Process shard awards when you complete app tests
- Facilitate the tester matching system between developers and testers
- Communicate platform-related updates via the ObsidianTesters Google Group
- Diagnose crashes and improve app performance via Firebase Crashlytics
- Prevent fraud and abuse of the shard economy

---

## 3. How We Store Your Information

Your data is stored in **Google Firebase** (Firestore database and Firebase Storage), operated by Google LLC. Data is stored on Google's secure servers. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you wish to have your data deleted, contact us at the email below.

App icon images you upload are stored in **Firebase Storage** under a path scoped to your user ID (`app_logos/{userId}/`).

---

## 4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

- **Publicly within the App** — Your app listings (app name, developer name, description, Play Store link, app icon) are visible to all signed-in users of the App.
- **Google Firebase** — As our backend infrastructure provider.
- **Google Play Billing** — For processing in-app purchases.
- **Firebase Crashlytics** — For crash reporting and diagnostics.
- **Legal Requirements** — If required by law or to protect our rights.
